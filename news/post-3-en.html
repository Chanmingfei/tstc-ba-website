Welcome Season: A Campus Life Guide Every Freshman Must Read

Class of 2026, welcome to Tangshan Normal University! Moving from high school to university, it is perfectly normal to feel both nervous and excited when facing an unfamiliar campus, dorm, and classmates. This guide bundles the most useful things for the start of term into one article—save it first and read it slowly on check-in day.

I. How to Get Around on Check-in Day

  • Confirm your college, major, and check-in location in the official school notice beforehand to avoid going to the wrong campus;
  • Bring all original documents—admission letter, ID card, file, and ID photos—in one document folder;
  • Park parent vehicles as directed; there are usually volunteers at the gate to guide you—ask any student in a vest if you get lost;
  • First complete registration at your college's reception point, then pick up your dorm key.

II. Dorm Life Tips

Your dorm is your "second home" for the next few years. We suggest agreeing with your roommates early on a schedule, cleaning rotation, and visitor rules—most conflicts build up from small things. High-power appliances and open flames are strictly forbidden, for everyone's safety.

A good first relationship with your roommates often starts with cleaning together.

III. The Big Deal: Food

The school cafeteria usually has several floors with different options for breakfast, lunch, and dinner; there are also snack streets and affordable restaurants nearby. It is normal for your appetite to need adjusting at the start—remember to eat three regular meals, stay up less, and drink more water.

IV. Getting Around and Packages

  • Getting around campus is mostly on foot; for distant teaching buildings, note the classroom numbers on your schedule;
  • Package pickup points are usually in fixed areas—bring your phone and pickup code when collecting;
  • For trips off campus, use licensed ride-hailing or public transit, and save your frequent addresses in your phone.

V. Don't Forget Us

When in doubt, feel free to post your questions on TSNU Bar, or @ the mod team in the bar. Scan the QR code on the homepage to join the QQ welcome group, where helpful seniors will answer online. University is long—don't be afraid to speak up.

May you meet the self you love at Tangshan Normal University.
