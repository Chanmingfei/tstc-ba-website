Bar Mod Team 2026 Recruitment Announcement

Hello everyone! The TSTC Bar serves as an online space for students across the school to connect and help one another, and we are committed to providing timely, useful campus information and a warm community atmosphere. To better serve our fellow bar members and maintain order in the bar, the Mod Team is now publicly recruiting from across the school. We welcome enthusiastic, responsible, and dedicated students to join us.

I. Open Positions

This recruitment primarily targets the following positions. You may choose based on your interests and availability:

  • Junior Bar Moderator: Assist with daily content management, maintain posting order, and answer questions from bar members;

II. Who We're Looking For

  • Share the Mod Team's original goal of "serving students and building the community together";
  • Be responsible, manage your time well, and maintain a basic level of online presence;
  • Communicate well and be willing to proactively collaborate when facing difficulties;
  • Those with skills in design, copywriting, video editing, etc. will be given priority, but it is by no means required.

III. How to Apply

Please submit your application through any of the following methods:

  1. Send an email to the Bar Master inbox at bazhu@tstc.pp.ua, with the subject line "Recruitment Application + Name + Preferred Position";
  2. Scan the QR code on the homepage, join the QQ welcome group, then message an admin privately;
  3. In the bar, @ any on-duty Junior Moderator and state your intention to apply.
Application period: From now until September 5, 2026. We will notify you of the interview arrangement via email or group message within one week after the application deadline.

IV. What You'll Gain

Joining the Mod Team is more than just taking on a "role"—it's gaining a group of like-minded friends and a campus experience of being needed and trusted. We provide official proof of work, regular skills training, and countless nights spent making the bar better together.

If you also want to leave a little light in the campus that belongs to you, then come join us. We're at the TSTC Bar, waiting for you.
