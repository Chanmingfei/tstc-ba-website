Freshman Guide (2) – Campus Dining Halls & Shopping

The university has two main campuses—Daxuedao and Xueyuanlu—and they differ significantly in dining hall distribution, on-campus stores, and nearby large shopping malls.

All information here is based on real on-campus observations. Dining and shopping are covered in one place; for a fuller picture, read it together with the previous article, "Is the Campus Card Worth Getting?"

I. Daxuedao Campus (156 Jianshe Road) Dining Guide

The campus is divided into two areas, South Yard and North Yard, with three dining halls in total. The three halls are fairly similar in menu and pricing, and all accept the campus one-card for payment.

1. North Yard New Dining Hall

This hall is relatively new and clean inside, but it sits near only a couple of North Yard dorm buildings, so most students have a long walk to and from meals.

2. South Yard First Dining Hall & Campus Life Hall

These two are the closest dining spots to the dorms on the whole campus, and the vast majority of South and North Yard students choose them daily.

  • First Dining Hall: right next to the teaching buildings, convenient for a meal on the way to and from class; it has self-service campus-card machines for top-ups, loss reporting, and card replacement, plus a separate ethnic-minority dining area on the south side.
  • Campus Life Hall: a mixed dining area with various food stalls and a small on-campus supermarket inside. But this store has limited selection and low value for money, so it is only good for grabbing small emergency items—not recommended for bulk stocking, fresh produce, or large household goods.

3. Two Major Supermarkets Near Daxuedao Campus (Top Pick for Bulk Shopping)

Besides the on-campus dining halls, there are two well-regarded local supermarkets near Daxuedao, ideal for one-stop bulk shopping.

  • Tangbai Supermarket (Basement 1, Renmin Shopping Plaza): a few hundred meters from campus, within walking distance. A long-established local Tangshan chain with a full range of grains, toiletries, snacks, and frozen fresh food at stable prices—great for one-time dorm stock-ups.
  • Zhengzhaofeng Fresh Supermarket (Basement 1, Yuanyangcheng Shopping Center): an extremely well-reviewed local fresh-food supermarket about 1 km from campus. It offers abundant fresh produce, prepared dishes, and daily goods; Yuanyangcheng is a full shopping mall where you can shop, watch movies, and dine after—a popular weekend destination.

II. Xueyuanlu Campus (41 Xueyuan North Road) Dining & Commercial District Guide

1. On-Campus Dining Overview

Xueyuanlu is the older campus with generally dated facilities; its dining hall is small and offers a limited variety of dishes.

2. Extra Dining Option: Nearby Merchant Meal-Order Groups

Merchants near campus run exclusive budget bento delivery groups—affordable and delivered right to campus, a handy backup when the dining hall gets repetitive.

3. Nearby Core Commercial District: Aegean Shopping Park

The campus sits in a bustling central district of Tangshan; a short walk reaches Aegean Shopping Park, whose basement houses the first Hema Fresh store in Hebei Province.

Hema features premium live seafood, same-day fresh produce, and prepared dishes, with fast online delivery to campus; the mall covers dining, clothing, and entertainment for one-stop shopping and strolling—the top choice for Xueyuanlu students to buy fresh food and unwind on weekends.

III. General Campus Living Tips (Linked to the Campus Card Guide)

  1. All on-campus dining halls accept only the campus card; no campus WiFi is needed to use them;
  2. Library, academic affairs system, and other on-campus network resources: accessible whether or not you get the Unicom campus card. Without the card you can use the school free VPN; the campus card is optional—beware of scams where people posing as seniors push data or campus cards;
  3. Shopping plan: for emergency small items, go to the Campus Life Hall mini-supermarket; for bulk daily goods and fresh stock, Daxuedao students choose Renmin Tangbai or Yuanyangcheng Zhengzhaofeng Fresh, while Xueyuanlu students head straight to Aegean Hema Fresh;
  4. Dining route reference: after class, pick the nearest First Dining Hall/Life Hall; the few North Yard dorm students can use the North Yard New Dining Hall; Xueyuanlu on-campus hall is limited, so join a nearby delivery group for more variety.
