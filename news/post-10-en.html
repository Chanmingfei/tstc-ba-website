Freshman Guide (V): Transportation & Travel

Tangshan Normal University has two campuses — Daxuedao Campus and Xueyuan Road Campus — and daily commuting and shopping trips differ considerably between them. Tangshan has no metro yet, but the city bus network is extensive, and both campuses are well served by public buses, making travel fairly convenient.

This article compiles routes from the stations to campus, travel between campuses, and trips to shopping areas. Read it together with the earlier dining and shopping guide to help freshmen quickly get the hang of getting around.

I. Getting to Campus (for Freshman Check-in)

On check-in day freshmen carry a lot of luggage. The routes below are organized by transportation hub; for bulky luggage we recommend taking a ride-hailing car first.

Tangshan Railway Station

  • To Daxuedao Campus (156 Jianshe Road): Take bus 22 or 26 to a stop near campus;
  • To Xueyuan Road Campus: Take bus 55 directly to the surrounding area.

For bulky luggage, we recommend taking a ride-hailing car first.

Tangshan North Station

  • To Daxuedao Campus: Take express bus D03, flat fare 5 yuan year-round;
  • With lots of luggage, prefer ride-hailing; regular bus routes take much longer.

Tangshan West Station

Take bus 97 directly to the area around Daxuedao Campus.

Tangshan Sannühe Airport

Take Airport Shuttle Line 2 to reach near Daxuedao Campus.

II. Commuting Between the Two Campuses

Daxuedao Campus ↔ Xueyuan Road Campus: bus 55 is the top choice, about 20 minutes one way in normal traffic.

Tip: Students at the two campuses basically never take classes across campuses; most trips are for errands. Plan your time and leave a buffer before traveling to deal with unexpected traffic jams.

III. Travel Tips for Nearby Shopping Areas

  • Daxuedao Campus: Nearby are Renmin Shopping Plaza and Ocean City;
  • Xueyuan Road Campus: Right next to Aegean Shopping Center.

The shopping areas are close to the campuses and are basically reachable on foot, so no bus or bike ride is needed.

IV. Tangshan Bus Payment Methods and Fare Rules

Standard city routes: 1 yuan in the non-air-conditioned season, 2 yuan in the air-conditioned season.

Note: Express buses like D03 and suburban routes have special fares — a flat 5 yuan for the whole trip.

Available payment methods:

  • Alipay Travel: Search "Tangshan Bus" to activate the e-ride code; after topping up the balance in the ride code you get a 10% discount;
  • WeChat: Open the "City Pass" mini-program, set location to Tangshan, and activate the bus ride code;
  • UnionPay Cloud Flash Pay: Use the ride code in the Travel section; the regular payment code also works directly for boarding;
  • e-CNY App: Activate the Tangshan Bus ride code in the Life Services section;
  • NFC: Any physical card or phone virtual card with the Transport Union logo can be tapped to ride;
  • Cash (coin into the box).

Transfer discount: Alipay, WeChat, and UnionPay Cloud Flash Pay ride codes support unlimited free transfers within 90 minutes, as long as the transfer route fare is no higher than the first ride's fare.

V. Important Reminders on Bikes

  • Shared bikes are not allowed inside campus; outside campus you can normally use shared bikes and shared e-bikes;
  • Park only in officially designated areas; random parking incurs a rebalancing fee;
  • For shared e-bikes, watch the service area to avoid running out of power mid-trip.

VI. Travel Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Prefer ride-hailing and avoid hailing street taxis directly — Tangshan local taxis are often known for overcharging;
  • Do not take private cars that solicit passengers at train or bus station exits — chaotic bargaining and no safety guarantee;
  • During holidays and the evening rush hour it is harder to get a car, so book in advance;
  • When going out alone at night, choose licensed ride-hailing or buses and avoid remote roads;
  • Most city buses have last departures between 21:30–22:00, so plan your return time wisely when out at night.

Extra Tips

For daily shopping: Daxuedao Campus students prefer Ocean City and Renmin Shopping; Xueyuan Road Campus students go to Aegean nearby. Just plan your route using this transportation guide.
