About Us

Connecting TNU members · A campus online community serving TNU members

Who We Are

The Tangshan Normal University Baidu Tieba (Tangshan Normal University Bar) is a campus online community built on the Baidu Tieba platform, serving as an important online platform for our faculty, students, and alumni to communicate and share information independently. Upholding the university motto of "diligent study and reflection, self-discipline and conduct," the Bar is dedicated to serving all TNU members and building a civilized, open, and supportive campus exchange space.

Platform Positioning & Functions

As a campus information hub and interactive exchange platform, the Bar covers multiple dimensions including academic development, campus life, freshman Q&A, employment and postgraduate exams, club activities, and second-hand trading, fully addressing the daily study and life needs of faculty and students.

Freshman Service Window

Every admissions season, the Bar becomes an important channel for prospective students to learn about the university. Faculty and students voluntarily share practical information such as campus environment, major introductions, dining and accommodation, and registration procedures, building the first bridge of communication between newcomers and their alma mater and helping them quickly adapt to university life.

Academic Exchange Platform

Faculty and students exchange ideas on topics such as coursework, postgraduate exam preparation, certification planning, and academic discussions, sharing study materials and preparation experience, fostering a supportive learning atmosphere and helping students grow academically.

Life Services Hub

It gathers lifestyle content such as surrounding campus information, dining and accommodation experiences, travel guides, lost and found, and second-hand exchanges, providing faculty and students with convenient and practical references for campus life.

Campus Culture Carrier

It synchronizes updates on major campus events, showcases the spirit of faculty and students, carries forward the university's humanistic spirit, and serves as an online extension and supplement to campus culture building.

Management & Operations

The Bar is independently managed by students, with a moderator team responsible for daily order maintenance, content moderation, and event organization. The moderator team strictly follows the Baidu Tieba agreement and relevant campus regulations, upholding the principles of openness, fairness, and transparency, advocating rational and civilized communication, continuously purifying the community environment, and fostering a healthy and positive discussion atmosphere.

The Bar adheres to the operational philosophy of student self-governance and serving faculty and students, developing in a standardized manner under the guidance of the university, preserving the vitality and openness of an online community while upholding the positive value orientation of a campus platform.

Vision

The Tangshan Normal University Baidu Tieba has always remained true to its original aspiration of connecting and serving TNU members, continuously optimizing the community ecosystem and improving service quality. In the future, it will further leverage the advantages of the online community, smooth the channels of communication between faculty and students, enrich campus cultural life, strengthen the emotional bonds among alumni, and strive to become a warm, profound, and inclusive spiritual home for all TNU members.
